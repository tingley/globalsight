Global Sight is an enterprise software start-up company that builds web globalization products. At Global Sight, we are dedicated to helping the world communicate with the use of multilingual/multicultural web sites. The company is well positioned to become the market leader in providing software solutions for management of multilingual content on the web. These products are necessary for global enterprises to cost effectively create, maintain and profitably manage a multilingual and multicultural web presence. Without such tools companies will not be able to reach the 700 million on-line non-English speakers forecasted by 2005. Without such tools companies will not be able to reach the forecasted $1.2 trillion in on line global transactions in 2001.
